How to use

  1. Step 1: Write your prompt.
  2. Step 2: Press enter or click the send button.
  3. Step 3: Read the output.
  4. Step 4: Rank the output if you want or edit it.
  5. Step 5: Continue the discussion untill you have no more queries.

Here are some tips and tricks to help you get the most out of this website:

  • Tip 1: Be specific with your questions.
  • Tip 2: The model needs to be tamed. So try to put it in the mood to answer your question.
  • Tip 3: The model can give helarious, false or completely made up responses. So be vigilant not to use the model for sensitive subjects like medication or financial advice

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

How to install?
Download the model from the github then use install.bat for windows and install.sh for linux/macOsX.
